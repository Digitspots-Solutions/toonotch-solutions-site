Our Services

Inverter & Solar Panel Installation

Complete solar power system design and installation for homes, offices, and industrial facilities. We supply and install high-efficiency solar panels with premium inverter systems that guarantee reliable backup power during outages.

Inverter Battery Solutions

Premium deep-cycle inverter batteries with extended lifespan and superior performance. We offer expert battery sizing, installation, and maintenance services to ensure your power system runs optimally.

Solar Street Lights

Eco-friendly solar-powered street lighting solutions for estates, streets, parks, and compounds. Our solar street lights provide bright, reliable illumination while reducing electricity costs to zero.

CCTV & Security Systems

Advanced surveillance camera installation for residential and commercial properties. Our CCTV systems feature high-definition recording, remote monitoring capabilities, and night vision technology to keep your property secure 24/7.

Maintenance & Support

Regular maintenance packages, system diagnostics, repairs, and upgrades. Our after-sales support ensures your investment continues delivering maximum value year after year.
